The opportunity & the gap

Africa holds the carbon. Conventional MRV can't reach it.

2.5–4 GtCO₂e/yr
Africa's nature-based solution potential by 2030 — yet under 5% is monetised today.
<10 %
of voluntary carbon supply comes from Africa — despite 30%+ of the world's tropical forest.
±25–40 %
Typical uncertainty from global biomass models in heterogeneous smallholder landscapes.

Field-centric MRV is too costly, too slow, and too inaccurate for fragmented African landscapes. The reliability of MRV is now the single greatest determinant of project credibility and commercial viability.

Platform architecture

One platform. Three engines. Three questions.

01 · MEASUREMENT
"What is the carbon?"
Multi-source EO + field-calibrated ML biomass models. GEDI LiDAR, Sentinel-1/2, allometric calibration.
→ tCO₂e/pixel · uncertainty · change
02 · CARBON ACCOUNTING
"What can be claimed?"
Codified VM0048 & multi-methodology logic: dynamic baseline, leakage, additionality, credit forecast.
→ baseline · net additionality · forecast
03 · COMPLIANCE
"Will it pass VVB review?"
AI-assisted additionality & permanence, FPIC, risk scoring, VVB dossier and AI PDD generation.
→ evidence · risk · dossier · PDD

What it takes to go live

Cloud & EO infrastructure
Provision GCP, GEE commercial licence, Cloud Run inference router, PostGIS + GCS data lake. Apply for Google for Startups credits.
02
Data partnerships
Contract Space Intelligence (HabitatMapper™/CarbonMapper™, VT0007) & Chloris. Fallback pipeline runs from day one — no blocker.
03
Engineering build
React web + FastAPI services + Python VM0048 methodology engine; offline mobile app; ML biomass training & inference.
04
Pilot project
Select one Kenya/Uganda ARR or REDD+ site with EO history & field access; run the full loop; gather calibration plots.
05
VVB & registry
Engage a VVB early on digital-MRV acceptability; design outputs to Verra/Gold Standard formats; set buffer pool.
06
Team & governance
Carbon leads, geospatial/ML engineers, field enumerators + QA, compliance reviewer; audit trails & version control throughout.
